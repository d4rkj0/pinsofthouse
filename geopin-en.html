📍 Places and parking utility

Geo Pin saves your places without complications.

Save your parking position, an important place, or a personal point of interest directly on the map. Geo Pin is designed to help you quickly find what you need, with no account, no complicated setup, and no wasted time.

Download on Google Play View images

How it works

Geo Pin helps you save and find your personal places. You can use your current position, add a reference on the map, and keep useful information directly on your device.

1

Open the app

No complicated setup: open it and start using it.

2

Save a place

Use your current position or choose a point on the map.

3

Add details

Add a name or note to quickly recognize the saved point.

4

Find it again

Open the map when you need it and easily reach the saved place.

Why use it

It is a small utility designed for anyone who wants to remember parking spots, useful places, and personal points without relying on memory.

  • Fast: open the app, save the point, and find it again when you need it.
  • Practical: ideal for parking spots, appointments, shops, visited places, and points of interest.
  • Visual: the map makes it easier to understand where each saved place is located.
  • Essential: no account required and data saved locally on the device.

Ideal for

City parking, travel, visited places, useful points to remember, or simple personal references. Basically: all those times when you think “I’ll remember this” and then the place turns into a tiny urban mystery.

  • 🚗 Parking spots and temporary stops
  • 📍 Personal places to remember
  • 🗺️ Points of interest during trips and daily travel
  • ⚡ Quick access to saved references
