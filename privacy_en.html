This Privacy Policy explains how Dungeon Thirteen handles user information. The game is designed to respect your privacy and does not require account registration.

Data Collection

Dungeon Thirteen does not collect, store, share, sell, or transmit personal data.

The game does not require your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.

Local Save Data

Dungeon Thirteen only uses local storage on the user's device to save game-related data.

This may include information such as game progress, settings, preferences, scores, or other gameplay-related data.

This data remains on your device and is not sent to external servers by the game. If you uninstall the game or clear the app data, this locally saved information may be deleted.

Internet Connection

Dungeon Thirteen does not require an online account and does not use external servers to store your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Dungeon Thirteen does not intentionally use third-party analytics, advertising systems, tracking tools, or social login services.

If the app is distributed through an app store, the store provider may process some information according to its own privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Dungeon Thirteen does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the game does not collect personal data, no personal information from children is stored or shared by the game.

Data Security

Since Dungeon Thirteen does not collect personal data, there is no personal user database managed by the game. Local game data is stored only on the user's device.
