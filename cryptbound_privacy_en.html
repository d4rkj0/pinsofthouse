Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for the game Cryptbound, developed by Giuseppe Maiello - Pin Soft House.

Last updated: May 18, 2026

In short: Cryptbound does not require registration or online accounts. The game only stores progress and settings locally on the user’s device.

1. Data Controller

Giuseppe Maiello - Pin Soft House
Contact email: pinsofthouse@gmail.com

2. Data Processed by the Game

Cryptbound does not collect personally identifiable information and does not use external servers to store user data.

The game only uses a local save system on the user’s device to store:

  • game progress;
  • local settings;
  • data necessary for the correct functioning of the game.

3. Purpose of Data Usage

Locally stored data is used exclusively to allow the game to function correctly, preserve player progress, and improve the gameplay experience.

4. Visual Effects and Content Warning

Cryptbound contains:

  • bright visual effects;
  • red particles used to simulate combat effects;
  • flashes, lighting effects, and color variations during gameplay.

These effects may cause discomfort for users who are sensitive to flashing lights, intense colors, or dynamic visual effects.

5. Data Sharing

The game does not automatically share personal data with third parties and does not use tracking, profiling, or online data collection systems.

6. Data Retention

Data remains stored exclusively on the user’s device until manually deleted by clearing the app data or uninstalling the game.

7. User Rights

Users may delete local game data at any time through their device settings.

For questions or requests, users may contact the developer using the email address listed above.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in case of changes to the game, its features, or any services that may be introduced in the future.
