Player Picker

In production

A simple and fun app designed to choose the first player. Just place two or more fingers on the screen, wait for the countdown, and the app randomly selects who starts first. It is useful for board game nights, party games, role-playing games, and any situation where you need a quick, clear, and fair way to decide who begins.

Scan the QR code Open the Player Picker listing directly on Google Play from your smartphone.

Geo Pin

In production

A practical app to save, organize, find, and share points of interest using your device location. It supports GPS coordinates, readable addresses when available, custom categories, data export/import, and sharing through messaging apps or Google Maps-compatible links.
