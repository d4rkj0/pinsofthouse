🎲 Board game utility

Player Picker chooses who starts without arguments.

Just place your fingers on the screen: the app starts the countdown and randomly selects the first player. It is designed for game nights, groups around the table, and matches where you want to start right away, without coins, dice, or tiny ritual arguments.

Download on Google Play View screenshots Italian version

How it works

Player Picker removes the classic “who starts?” question with an instant solution: each player touches the screen, the app detects the touches and picks a winning color after a short countdown.

1

Open the app

No complicated setup: open it and you are ready to go.

2

Everyone touches

From 2 to 6 players place a finger on the screen.

3

The countdown starts

The app automatically starts the selection.

4

First player

A color is chosen at random and the game can begin.

Why use it

It is a small utility created for board game lovers who want a quick, clear, and fun way to choose the first player.

  • Fast: open the app, touch the screen, and immediately find out who starts.
  • Group-friendly: supports games with 2 to 6 players.
  • Visual: each finger is linked to a bright, easy-to-recognize color.
  • Essential: no account, no ads, and no personal data required.

Ideal for

Board game nights, party games, quick fillers, family games, or evenings at a game club. Basically: every time the rulebook says “choose the first player randomly” and nobody feels like inventing a ridiculous criterion.

  • 🎲 Modern board games
  • 🃏 Card games and party games
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family games
  • ⚡ Faster setup before starting
